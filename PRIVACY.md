# Privacy Policy

**iMuJoCo** is an open-source physics simulation app. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

iMuJoCo does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

## Network Usage

iMuJoCo uses **local network (UDP)** only for communicating with external simulation drivers on the same network. No data is sent to remote servers or third parties.

## Analytics

iMuJoCo does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

iMuJoCo does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Changes

If this policy changes, updates will be posted in this repository.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/hhkblogi/iMuJoCo/issues.
